Frequently Ask Questions

Can I accept both Paypal and Stripe?

Yes, MyCargoRoute supports both Paypal and Stripe as payment options. You can choose the one that best suits your preferences and needs.

What is the available refund period?

We offer a 30-day refund period from the date of your purchase. If you are unsatisfied with our services, you can request a refund within this timeframe.

How can I track my shipments?

Tracking your shipments is easy! Simply log in to your MyCargoRoute account and go to the "Track Shipment" section. Enter your tracking number, and you'll get real-time updates on your cargo's location and status.

What is your customer support availability?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you. You can reach out to us through our contact form, email, or phone, and we'll promptly address your inquiries and concerns.

How can I customize my shipping preferences?

You can easily customize your shipping preferences in your account settings. Navigate to the "Preferences" section, where you can specify delivery options, packaging requirements, and more according to your needs.

What is your opening time?

Our offices are open from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Feel free to reach out during these hours for any assistance or inquiries you may have.

Do you provide international shipping services?

Absolutely! MyCargoRoute specializes in international shipping services. Whether it's across borders or continents, we ensure your cargo reaches its destination securely and on time.
